Our Donate Plan

As long as there is such a tragedy in our country, we will be by their side.

We will help them as much as we can.

Food for a family

We will continue to provide dry food among the Bannarthas.Not only dry food. We will continue to deliver nutritious food to them as much as we can.

Shelter for a family

In the flood, many families' houses were submerged in water. They need shelter. We will rescue them with our rescue team and take them to a safe place.

Education for a child

During the flood, there is no school for the children to study because there is water all around. We will continue to teach them through Brahmaman School.

Why Donate?

An estimated 7.2 million people have been severely affected following devastating flash floods which began in May 2022 with a second wave starting around the 15 June 2022 in nine northeastern districts of Bangladesh: Sylhet, Sunamganj, Moulvibazar, Habiganj, Kishorganj, Netrakona, Brahmanbaria, Mymensing and Sherpur.Among the nine districts, the five districts that are most heavily impacted are Sylhet, Sunamganj, Moulivazar, Habiganj and Netrakona.
